The Pillars of Catt Kaizen Transformational Coaching


Mindset

What kind of thoughts have you been projecting into the world? The ability to think constructively will always be the first pillar in any experience of transformation. Control of your mind and thoughts is the foundation upon which everything else is built.

 Manifestation

Do you understand that everything in your life, you created? We can entertain ourselves by reading about the "Law of Attraction" and doubting it, but when we can really see the truths behind it, we become powerfully responsible in life.

 Boundaries

How strong are the boundaries within? People with empowered boundaries do not need to ask people to respect their boundaries, they are innate and emanate from within. Boundaries determine the quality of life that flows through you.

Spirituality

Do you know how incredible your life is meant to be? Connecting spiritually is important, but may be one of the last things to come in place. Keeping an open mind about spirituality is imperative for transformation.
